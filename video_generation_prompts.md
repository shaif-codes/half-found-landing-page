Use these prompts to generate 2-3 second cinematic animations for each key screen of the app. Match the **VibeTalk/Half Found** dark theme: Primary `#ee2b8c` (Pink), Background `#221019`.

---

**Prompt:**

### Tips for Best Quality:
- **Aspect Ratio**: Use 9:16 for a native phone look.
- **Motion Level**: Low (for UI focus).
- **Style**: Choose "3D Render" or "Cinematic" if available in Flow Labs.
